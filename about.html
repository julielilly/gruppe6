About

  • Vild Mad is an initiative that is about the wild, edible nature and how you can bring it into the kitchen. In the whole country.
  • We believe that using wild ingredients in the kitchen can be part of a rich and futureistic lifestyle, so we have developed a number of tools and guides that can help you get better at both finding them and using them in the kitchen. Since 2015, more than 100 people – from chefs and gatherers to academics and designers – have helped build an ambitious resource for anyone interested. A large handful of organizations have shared all their knowledge and skills in the process – learn more about each one by clicking on the logos at the bottom of the page.
  • Download the Vild Mad app, a tool that helps you explore the Danish nature. Explore the diverse landscapes, find the delicious ingredients and use the inventive recipes in the kitchen.
  • Explore our teaching material for Danish schools. We have adapted it to the requirements and framework of the new school reform, so that it is as easy as possible to access. Contact one of our Vild Mad mediators if you want a breath of fresh air in your teaching.
